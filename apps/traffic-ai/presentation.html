PROBLEM STATEMENT

Static Signals in a Dynamic City

Urban traffic changes minute by minute, but most intersections still rely on fixed-timer plans or manual overrides. The result is avoidable congestion, inefficient signal cycles, and delayed emergency response. Cities need real-time visibility and adaptive control that can scale across intersections.

  • Lane-by-lane demand is ignored, creating unnecessary queues and spillback.
  • Emergency vehicles have no automatic priority and lose critical time at red lights.
  • Operators lack a single live view of traffic density and signal performance.
ST

STATIC TIMING

Fixed cycles do not reflect real traffic demand, causing idle greens in empty lanes and long waits in busy ones.

EMS

EMERGENCY DELAYS

Ambulances and fire trucks depend on manual coordination instead of automated corridor clearance.

DATA

LIMITED INSIGHT

Traffic decisions are often based on periodic surveys rather than live, high-resolution analytics.

03 / 08
SOLUTION

Traffic AI Optimizer

Traffic AI Optimizer delivers an end-to-end pipeline that connects computer vision directly to signal control. YOLOv8 detects vehicles from video feeds, weighted density metrics translate counts into congestion levels, and an adaptive signal optimizer allocates green time fairly across lanes. When an emergency vehicle is detected, the system computes the fastest route and activates a green corridor automatically.

CV

REAL-TIME VEHICLE DETECTION

YOLOv8 + OpenCV classify cars, buses, trucks, and motorcycles and stream annotated frames to the dashboard.

AI

ADAPTIVE SIGNAL TIMING

Density-driven optimizer distributes green time proportionally with minimum guarantees and severe-congestion priority.

ROUTE

EMERGENCY GREEN CORRIDOR

Emergency detection triggers shortest-path routing and signal overrides along the corridor to speed response.

OPS

OPERATOR CONTROL CENTER

React and Streamlit dashboards provide live maps, signal plans, video ingestion, and one-click demos.

DENSITY CALCULATOR

PCU-weighted load estimation and congestion classification for single lanes and intersections.

Proportional green allocation with minimum guarantees and severe-congestion priority, plus rotation sequences.
